### What are exhibitions at Lincoln High School?
Every year, every student at Lincoln (sometimes freshman are excluded, they go back and forth) presents a project at the school, to the entire rest of the school. There's 3 options for a project you can present.

1. Present a Project That You Made in Class
    - Easiest Option, You Use a Previously Made Class Project.
    - You Present This with Your Class and Can Easily Have Someone Else Help.
    - Often You're Not the Proudest of This Work, But It's Already Done So...
  
2. Present a Project for a Club / Sport
    - Middle Option, You Show Off Something From Your Club / Sport and then Talk About It.
    - You Hang Out with Your Club / Team and Try to Get People Interested, and Maybe Recruit Them.
    - Very Fun, Still Not Really *Fully Yours.*

3. **Personal Project (My Pick)**
    - Hardest Option, It's ALL YOU (or A Small Group).
    - YOU Decide What to Share
    - YOU Decide How to Share it
    - YOU Have to Figure Out How

## What is **My Exhibition?**

My Goal is to Demonstrate & Educate People on How Gearing Systems Work and Affect The World

I am going to create and explain models of 
- 3 types of Differential (Open, Welded/Direct, Torsen)
- 2 types of Gearbox (Standard Manual, CVT)

and I am going to give out 3d-printed miniature versions of the open diff models.
